How to use this map

This map shows potential flood exposure based on terrain and drainage modeling. It does NOT represent historical flooding or FEMA insurance zones.

Geographic scope:
This map covers only the central San José study area shown. It does not represent upstream or downstream flood behavior.

  1. Colored street segments indicate portions of streets intersecting modeled surge pathways.
  2. Color shows relative exposure:
    • Purple / Red – Higher exposure
    • Blue – Moderate exposure
    • Green – Lower exposure
  3. Only the colored portions of a street are affected.
  4. Blue lines show rivers and drainage channels (hydrology), not flood severity.
  5. “Lower” risk means lower relative exposure — not zero risk.
Datasets used
  • High-resolution Digital Elevation Model (DEM)
  • National Hydrography Dataset (NHD/NHDPlus) – rivers & drainage
  • OpenStreetMap (OSM) – street geometry
  • Derived terrain flow & accumulation surfaces

Intended for situational awareness and planning only. Not for insurance, permitting, or regulatory use.
